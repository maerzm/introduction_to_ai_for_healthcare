An Introduction to AI for Healthcare Students

 

Welcome to this introductory series on AI for students in healthcare professions.

 

This series offers a comprehensive introduction to artificial intelligence (AI) in the context of healthcare. While it is not exhaustive or in-depth, it serves as a starting point to provide foundational knowledge necessary for understanding and engaging with AI in professional practice.

 

We’ll begin with a high-level summary of key concepts every healthcare professional should know about how AI is reshaping medicine. Whether you’re a doctor, nurse, or administrator, understanding these core principles will help you engage with AI systems, ask the right questions, and ensure ethical and effective use of AI in your practice.

 

This summary will guide you to specific chapters for a deeper dive into each topic. These chapters offer more detailed yet accessible information, helping you build a comprehensive understanding of AI in healthcare if you wish to explore further.
